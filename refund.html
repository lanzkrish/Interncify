Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: September 03, 2024

Please read this Refund and Cancellation policy carefully before using Our Service.

At Interncify, our goal is to ensure that our programs and services meet your expectations. We strive to provide high-quality training, internship opportunities, and educational resources. However, due to the nature of our services, we have a strict refund and cancellation policy.

1. No Refunds

Once payment is made for any of our services, including training programs, internships, or other educational resources, it is non-refundable. Please review all details and requirements carefully before making a payment.

2. Program Cancellation

If Interncify cancels a program, participants will be notified as soon as possible, and alternative arrangements may be offered. However, in the case of such cancellation if no alternatives can be provided then, a refund will be issued with in 7 days after the notification.

3. Changes to Programs

Interncify reserves the right to make changes to programs, schedules, or content as needed. These changes do not entitle participants to a refund or cancellation.
